About Smart college Helpdesk

Making complaint management simple, fast, and transparent.

What is this website

The Smart College Helpdesk System is a modern web-based platform designed to streamline the process of reporting and resolving campus-related issues. It provides a structured and efficient communication channel between students and administration.

Students can easily submit complaints, track their status in real-time, and receive updates and college or schools notices, while administrators can manage, prioritize, and resolve issues through a centralized dashboard.

The system focuses on improving transparency, reducing response time, and enhancing overall campus management through a simple and user-friendly interface.

How It Works

1.User Login

students and administrators securely log in using their credentials based on their roles.

2. Raise Complaint

students fill out a simple form with relevent details such as issue type,description,and priority.

3. Track Status

Check resolution progress.

3. Admin Action

admins analyze the issue,update its status,and take necessary actions for resolution.

3. Track And Resolve

students can track progress in real-time and receive updates until the issue is resolved.
